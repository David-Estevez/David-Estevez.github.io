RoboFactory

2018-2020, Robotics workgroup

RoboFactory is a workgroup within the UC3M Robotics Society (ASROB).

It was initially created as a way to introduce newcomers to the basic knowledge required to build robots, develop some robots for Robot Devastation and make use of the ton of materials that we have sitting around in our lab.

Currently it serves as a general group in which people with different projects that might now have enough people to become an independent work group can share a common space and help each other.
