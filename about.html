David Estévez

David is a researcher and artist based in Madrid. He holds a Ph.D in Robotics and Artificial Intelligence from the University Carlos III of Madrid.

His previous research works include teaching a humanoid robot to perform laundry tasks such as ironing or folding garments, the development of an augmented/mixed-reality robot game that addresses the physical limitations of robots for entertainment, and a self-organizing modular robot that learns different locomotion gaits for different morphologies using Differential Evolution and selects the most suitable movement coordinating modules through digital hormones.

Currently collaborates with artist Cristina de Propios researching the artistic possibilities of Deep Learning-based Artificial Intelligence and Artificial Life techniques applied to different artistic projects.
